Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

We collect various types of personal data to provide and improve our services. This includes data you provide directly and data collected through your interactions with our website.

1.1. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and browsing behavior. This helps us understand how you use our website and improve your experience.

1.2. Information You Provide

When you register for an account or contact us, we collect information such as your name, email address, and any other details you provide. This information is used to manage your account and respond to your inquiries.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide and enhance our services, communicate with you, and ensure that your experience with us is satisfactory. This includes using your data for service-related communications and marketing purposes with your consent.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with third parties for various reasons, such as to comply with legal obligations or to provide services. We ensure that any third parties we share data with have appropriate measures in place to protect your data.

4. Your Data Protection Rights

You have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your information. If you have any questions or concerns about your data, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at privacy@imaranetworks.com.
